Beef Heart Kebabs

Description

Beef hearts... The first time I saw one, the large size was intimidating. But, a grilled heart is one of the best selections for the plate. These spicy heart kebabs will do the trick.

Ingredients

  • 1 beef heart
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds or 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 chipotle peppers
  • 3 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • About 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 250 degrees F (121 C).
  2. Place the ribs in a deep pan.
  3. Cover the ribs with the BBQ rub.
  4. Mix BBQ sauce and cider. Brush the mixture onto the ribs.
  5. Cover the pan with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake for 3 hours. Brush BBQ sauce/cider onto ribs each hour.
  7. Remove the ribs from the oven.
  8. Coat the ribs with BBQ sauce and cider.
  9. Grill for 10-15 to desired level of char. Brush on last layer of BBQ sauce and cider when nearly finished grilling.
