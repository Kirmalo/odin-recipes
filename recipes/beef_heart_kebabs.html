Beef Heart Kebabs

Description

Beef hearts... The first time I saw one, the large size was intimidating. But, a grilled heart is one of the best selections for the plate. These spicy heart kebabs will do the trick.

Ingredients

  • 1 beef heart
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tablespoon cumin seeds or 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 chipotle peppers
  • 3 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • About 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a cast iron skillet to medium heat.
  2. Place the garlic in the pan and cook until brown on all sides.
  3. Put the cooked garlic in a small bowl.
  4. Toast the cumin in the skillet over medium heat until fragrant, 1-2 minutes.
  5. Place the cumin in the bowl with the garlic and add the chipotle peppers.
  6. Add the paprika and puree to a paste. Add the vinegars and enough oil to make a thick paste. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Cut beef hearts into strips about 1.5 inches wide. The strips should be about 2 inches long, 1.5 inches wide, and .5 inch thick.
  8. Weave pieces of meat onto the skewers so that the flat side is exposed to the fire.
  9. Put the skewers in a glass baking dish and spread the marinade over the meat with a spoon. Cover and marinate 6 to 12 hours, turning several times for full coverage.
  10. Heat the grill to high and oil the grill grate to preven sticking.
  11. Drain the marinade off the kebabs, and throw out the marinade.
  12. Grill until brown and cooked through (about 10 minutes).
  13. Baste on the grilled side with achiote or olive oil after 3 minutes to keep the meat moist.
  14. Serve hot!
